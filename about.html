Our Story

Built Around One Principle:
Movement Is Medicine.

Steady Physical Therapy was founded with a simple belief — that every patient deserves direct, expert care from a Doctor of Physical Therapy who's fully invested in their recovery.

Who We Are

A Solo Practice Built on Expertise, Not Volume

Steady Physical Therapy is a solo practice in the heart of Van Nuys, CA — and that's intentional. As the owner, lead therapist, and sole provider, I see every patient personally. No handoffs to aides or assistants. No 45-minute wait while someone else finishes. Just focused, expert, one-on-one care from the moment you walk in.

My approach is built on the latest movement science and pain research. I believe in treating the whole person — not just the injury — and empowering patients with the knowledge and tools to stay healthy long after they leave my clinic.

Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, or trying to get back in the game after a sports injury, Steady PT is where Van Nuys comes to move better.

Credentials & Expertise

Trained. Certified. Ready to Help.

Our qualifications go beyond a degree. We combine advanced clinical training with ongoing education in movement science, pain research, and injury rehabilitation.

Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT)

Advanced clinical doctorate with comprehensive training in musculoskeletal, neurological, and cardiopulmonary physical therapy.

Certified Movement Specialist

Specialized certification in human movement assessment and dysfunction correction — identifying the root cause of pain and injury patterns.

Pain & Injury Expert

Advanced training in pain neuroscience and modern pain management approaches, helping patients understand and overcome persistent pain.

Workers' Comp Specialist

Experienced in treating and documenting work-related injuries under California Workers' Compensation, supporting return-to-work programs.

Orthopedic & Sports Rehab

Deep specialization in orthopedic and sports injuries — from amateur athletes to post-surgical patients — with evidence-based return-to-sport protocols.

Personal Injury & MVA

Expert in motor vehicle accident rehabilitation and personal injury documentation, working alongside legal teams to support patient outcomes and claims.

Our Approach

Movement Is the Medicine. We're the Guide.

We don't believe in passive treatment. The research is clear: active participation, movement education, and progressive rehabilitation produce better outcomes than passive modalities alone. That's the foundation of everything we do at Steady PT.

Every treatment plan at Steady PT is built around three pillars: assess accurately, treat effectively, and educate thoroughly. You'll leave each session understanding your body better than when you arrived.
