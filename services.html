Physical Therapy Services
in Van Nuys, CA

Comprehensive, evidence-based physical therapy for every stage of recovery — from acute injuries to long-term pain management and performance. All treatment delivered directly by our Doctor of Physical Therapy.

Most Common

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Whether you're dealing with a joint injury, muscle tear, or bone-related condition, our orthopedic rehab program is designed to restore full function and eliminate pain. We use evidence-based techniques combined with hands-on manual therapy to accelerate your recovery.

  • Knee pain, ACL/MCL injuries & replacements
  • Shoulder impingement, rotator cuff tears
  • Hip pain & labral injuries
  • Ankle sprains & fractures
  • Back & neck pain
  • Wrist & elbow conditions

Sports Injuries

Active people need a therapist who understands the demands of sport and physical performance. We treat weekend warriors, high school athletes, and competitive players with return-to-sport protocols that are both safe and aggressive.

  • Sprains, strains & muscle tears
  • Tendinitis & overuse injuries
  • Runner's knee & IT band syndrome
  • Plantar fasciitis
  • Shoulder instability & labral tears
  • Return-to-sport performance training

Surgical Recovery

Post-Operative Recovery

Surgery is only half the journey. The right post-op rehab determines how well you recover, how quickly you return to activity, and whether you achieve lasting results. We work closely with your surgeon's protocols while individualizing every session to your progress.

  • ACL, meniscus & knee replacement surgery
  • Rotator cuff & shoulder reconstruction
  • Hip replacement & labral repair
  • Spinal surgery recovery
  • Foot & ankle surgical rehab
  • Total joint arthroplasty

Long-Term Relief

Chronic Pain Management

Living with chronic pain doesn't have to be your new normal. We take a whole-body, movement-science approach to identify the root causes of persistent pain and build a path toward long-term relief. Our treatment goes beyond symptom management — we help you understand and resolve the underlying dysfunction.

  • Chronic low back & neck pain
  • Fibromyalgia & widespread pain
  • Arthritis & joint degeneration
  • Headaches & TMJ dysfunction
  • Nerve pain & sciatica
  • Myofascial pain syndrome

Stability & Function

Balance & Mobility Training

Balance and mobility decline with age, injury, and inactivity — but they can be rebuilt at any stage of life. Our specialized training programs address the neurological, muscular, and vestibular components of balance to improve your stability, coordination, and overall quality of movement.

  • Fall prevention for older adults
  • Post-stroke balance rehabilitation
  • Vestibular disorders & vertigo
  • Parkinson's disease mobility
  • Gait dysfunction & walking abnormalities
  • Post-injury mobility restoration

Workers' Comp Accepted

Work-Related Accident Rehabilitation

Workplace injuries require specialized care and careful documentation. We are experienced in treating work-related injuries under Workers' Compensation and work to safely return you to your job as quickly and thoroughly as possible. We coordinate with your employer, insurance provider, and legal team as needed.

  • Repetitive strain & overuse injuries
  • Lifting & back injuries
  • Slip, trip & fall injuries
  • Carpal tunnel & wrist conditions
  • Shoulder & neck strain
  • Functional capacity evaluations

Personal Injury

Motor Vehicle Accident Rehabilitation

We provide thorough evaluation, treatment documentation, and evidence-based rehabilitation to support your physical recovery and your personal injury case.
